Graphic & Logo Design Questionnaire

Your brand and everything that includes or surrounds your brand should resonate with your mission, be pleasing to the eye, give a taste of your values, connect with your clients, be recognizable, and many other important (and often overlooked) details.

This Graphic & Logo Design Questionnaire asks you a lot of questions about you and your business and helps you think about all the things you want to portray with your brand and logo, including your design preferences.

If you don’t know the answer to something, you can leave it blank (there’s no need to stress over anything in here). This should be a fun and informative experience, so we hope you enjoy it.
