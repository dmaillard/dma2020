Our Manifesto

Who are we

David Maillard Design is a boutique web design and development company that builds unique, creative, professional, mobile responsive websites. The websites we build are search engine optimized and built with your goals, scalability, and success as our guide. We are based in Minas Gerais, Brazil and work with clients all over the world.

What we do

We help entrepreneurs grow their business, make connections with their clients, provide solutions to current challenges, and deliver increased functionality along with ease of use with a website that works seamlessly.

What makes us different

We take the time to learn as much as possible about our clients’ business so that we can create and maintain an online home for them that converts visitors into customers and keeps them coming back over and over again. From initial discovery calls to detailed questionnaires to project summaries and outlines, we dive deep into our clients’ goals and desired outcomes so we can build a product that is exactly what they want and need while always keeping in mind user experience, industry standards, and current trends. You can also expect exclusive client-only access to resources, continued support, and exceptional service.

Who we serve

From one-person mobile businesses to brick-and-mortar shops, we work with a variety of people who love the freedom of working for themselves and/or managing a team. Our clients are committed to their business goals, personal values, and giving back to the community.

We promise

You can count on us to always be honest, transparent, and your partner – equally committed to seeing your business thrive.

Why we do it

We do this because we believe in entrepreneurship and all of the advantages, freedoms, and satisfaction that comes along with it. We also love helping others achieve their dreams. We know anything is possible when you dream and believe.
